ABOUT

Madsir Studio represents a collection of radness and madness created by H B Bird.

Bird is a game developer with years of experience developing games and digital assets from conception to completion, and an artist with over a decade of experience creating digital and traditional artwork.

Current focuses include:
• Creating, developing and designing entire game worlds from conception to inception.
• Conceptualizing and designing textures and applying them to 2D objects and 3D models.
• Creating artwork for sprite sheets, slicing sprites, and applying them to Mecanim to create animations in Unity.
• Utilizing knowledge of game theory, composition, and artistic experience to design levels and compliment them with environmental artwork in order to build tangible 2D and 3D worlds.
• Character Design, Object Design, & UI Design

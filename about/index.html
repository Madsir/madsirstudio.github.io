ABOUT

Madsir Studio represents a collection of radness and madness created by H B Bird.


VIDEO GAMES

A good mix so far: A procedurally generated, infinite arcade-style platformer for PC & Android, and two casual math-practice games. Currently developing Remote Agent, an adventure platformer.



DIGITAL ASSETS

A set of tools to assist you in easily adding creative aspects to your Unity games. Currently available tools include dialog, subtitles, localization, dynamic string interpolation, sound management, and cinematics.

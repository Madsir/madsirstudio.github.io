Toot your way through the spacescapes. Eat floating space jellies to refill your gas tank.



Beware!



These spacescapes contain meteors flying in from all angles, threatening to knock you off course.

You'll run into glowing effectors which abruptly change the direction of gravity, and you'll come across drifting hourglasses which slow down or speed up the scale of time.



Levels



Complete all four levels to unlock the Space Toots Generator, capable of generating millions of unique spacescapes.



Score



Saves your highest score from each level, plus your highest score from a Space Toots Generator level. Score can be found in the main menu.



Settings



Visual settings include exposure and contrast controls as well as Calm Mode, a low visual intensity option.

Audio settings include volume control and a mute option.



Gameplay instructions included. Gamepad compatible.
